Theorem sylbbr 155
Description: A mixed syllogism inference from two biconditionals.

Note on the various syllogism-like statements in set.mm. The hypothetical syllogism syl 17 infers an implication from two implications (and there are 3syl 18 and 4syl 19 for chaining more inferences). There are four inferences inferring an implication from one implication and one biconditional: sylbi 137, sylib 138, sylbir 154, sylibr 153; four inferences inferring an implication from two biconditionals: sylbb 139, sylbbr 155, sylbb1 156, sylbb2 157; four inferences inferring a biconditional from two biconditionals: bitri 179, bitr2i 180, bitr3i 181, bitr4i 182 (and more for chaining more biconditionals). There are also closed forms and deduction versions of these, like, among many others, syl5 41, syl6 42, bitrid 184, bitrdi 183 and variants. (Contributed by BJ, 21-Apr-2019.)
